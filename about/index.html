About the project

Less noise.
Better questions.

SWO Guide is an independent learning companion for junior officers preparing to join the surface fleet. It turns broad, stressful questions into practical next actions while keeping official guidance where it belongs: first.

Editorial standard

Every guide is written to be useful without presenting itself as command direction. Time-sensitive claims should link to an official public source and carry a visible review date. Corrections are welcome at kevin.kim@swoguide.com.

What will never belong here

SWO Guide does not request or publish classified information, controlled unclassified information, ship movements, schedules, access details, watchbills, personal information, or command-specific material that should not be public.

Independence

SWO Guide is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official website of the U.S. Navy or the Department of Defense. Users must follow current official instructions and their chain of command.
