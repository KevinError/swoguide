What to send

  • The URL of the page you are writing about
  • A short description of the correction or suggestion

  • Enough context to understand the issue without sensitive details

Email kevin.kim@swoguide.com. SWO Guide does not provide official orders, legal advice, personnel support, emergency assistance, or command-specific direction.

Do not send sensitive information

Keep it public and unclassified

Do not send classified information, controlled unclassified information, operational details, schedules, movements, vulnerabilities, ship-specific procedures, medical information, personnel records, passwords, or personally identifying information.

If your concern involves safety, misconduct, security, health, or an urgent personnel issue, use the appropriate command or official support channel rather than this website.
