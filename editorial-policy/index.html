Editorial Policy

SWO Guide turns public information into practical starting points while keeping official direction, command authority, and operational security clearly in view.

Priority

Accuracy, usefulness, clarity, and safety

Questions

Contact the editor

Independence

SWO Guide is an independent educational website. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official publication of the U.S. Navy or Department of Defense. Content does not replace orders, regulations, PQS, command instructions, schoolhouse material, or direction from the chain of command.

Sources and verification

Guides prioritize current, public, primary sources such as official Navy, Department of Defense, and federal publications. When a practical recommendation is an editorial interpretation rather than an official requirement, the article is written to make that distinction clear.

Details that can vary by command, platform, location, course, or date are framed as questions to verify—not universal rules.

Review and updates

Articles display a review date and are revisited when an official source changes, a reader identifies a credible issue, or the topic needs clarification. A review date means the article was checked; it does not guarantee that every linked external page will remain available.

Corrections

Readers can report errors through the Contact page. Corrections are evaluated using public evidence. Material errors are corrected promptly, and the review information is updated when the change affects the substance of the guide.

Safety, privacy, and operational security

SWO Guide intentionally avoids classified, controlled, ship-specific, or operationally sensitive detail. We do not solicit internal courseware, watchbills, schedules, movements, vulnerabilities, personnel information, answer keys, or restricted material.

Official channels come first

Urgent safety, security, legal, medical, personnel, or misconduct concerns should be reported through the appropriate command or official support channel—not submitted to this website.

Advertising and commercial relationships

Advertising or affiliate relationships, if introduced, will be identified and will not determine editorial conclusions. Payment will not buy favorable coverage or alter a correction decision.

Scope

The site is designed to help new Surface Warfare Officers organize questions and prepare to learn. It does not certify qualifications, assess fitness for duty, or offer individualized professional advice.
