Privacy policy

SWO Guide does not ask visitors to create an account or submit personal information through the website. If you contact SWO Guide by email, the information you send is used to respond to your message, maintain necessary records, and address corrections or safety concerns unless the law requires otherwise.

Google Analytics

SWO Guide uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate website traffic and improve its guides. Google Analytics may collect information such as pages visited, approximate location, device and browser information, referral source, and interactions with the site. It may use cookies or similar technologies. Google processes this information under its own terms and privacy practices.

Learn more through Google’s Privacy Policy and Google Analytics privacy information.

Google AdSense and advertising

SWO Guide uses Google AdSense connection technology to verify the site and, after approval, may display Google-served advertising. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, IP addresses, and similar technologies to deliver, measure, secure, and personalize ads where permitted. Ads may not appear while the site is under review.

Where required, visitors will be offered consent choices before advertising technologies are used. You can review how Google uses information for advertising and manage advertising preferences through My Ad Center.

Your choices

You can limit or delete cookies through your browser settings. Google also provides a Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Browser privacy settings or extensions may also restrict analytics collection.

Operational and sensitive information

Do not send classified information, controlled unclassified information, ship movements, schedules, access details, watchbills, medical records, Social Security numbers, DoD identification numbers, or other sensitive information through this website or by email.
