Your opening moves

The first 30 days,
in three phases.

A calm sequence for turning an unfamiliar ship into a place where you can learn, contribute, and lead.

01Before you report

Arrive ready to learn

Confirm your sponsor, organize your documents, and build a simple system for capturing names, acronyms, and questions.

02Days 1–7

Learn the ship’s rhythm

Meet your chain of command, walk your spaces, understand duty expectations, and listen before changing anything.

03Days 8–30

Turn notes into a plan

Set a qualification cadence, learn your division’s work, and build reliable communication with your chief and Sailors.
